About Us

Welcome to Stay Assist – your one-stop solution for hassle-free living in college! We understand the everyday challenges students face while managing accommodation and essential services. Our platform simplifies the process by connecting you with trusted service providers for electric repairs, furniture fixes, laundry, room cleaning, and pest control.

At Stay Assist, we aim to create a seamless experience by offering a user-friendly interface that lets you request services effortlessly. Whether it's a power outage, a broken chair, or a laundry emergency, we’ve got you covered!

Our mission is to enhance student living by making service booking quick, reliable, and efficient. With just a few clicks, you can schedule your needs and get professional help at your doorstep.

Your comfort, our priority!
